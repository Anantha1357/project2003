TIPS for being healthy and hygiene

Hygiene keeps us

  • Hygiene deals with various practices, principles or habits that help in maintaining health.
  • The ability of the body to fight the disease causing organisms is known as immunity
  • A physically healthy person is energetic, alert and is able to perform his/her jobs well.

    • Steps to Improve Hygiene:

  • Every day, take a bath. Dust particles adhere to our skin as we play or sweat. Bacteria that cause illness are attracted to these dust particles.
  • Brushing twice a day and flossing on a regular basis will help to remove food particles that are stuck or caught between our teeth .
  • Trim your nails on a regular basis. Keep your hair clean and lice-free.
  • Wash your hands before and after each meal. Germs from the dirt and mud are conveyed to our hands while we play, and they can enter our bodies and cause illness.
  • Daily bathing helps to keep hazardous microorganisms at bay. Keep your eyes and ears in good condition .


    • **Things to Remember**

    The term "health" refers to the body's natural and healthy state. Hygiene refers to healthy habits that help to prevent sickness and maintain excellent health. Approximately 90% of office mugs contain germs and bacteria that can get you sick. So make sure to wash your utensils before using them. Did you know that the germs and other particles released by sneezing can travel up to 130 kilometres per hour? Always remember to cover your sneeze. A balanced and nutritious diet is a prerequisite for good health. Fruits and vegetables have vital minerals and vitamins which we need. We must wash fruits and cook vegetables before consumption. Hygiene refers to conditions and practises that help to maintain health and prevent the spread of diseases..
